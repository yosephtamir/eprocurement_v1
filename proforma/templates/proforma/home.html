Gafarsa Guje Sub-City, led by Mr. Ashenafi Chemeda, encompasses 17,130 hectares with a population of 271,565. It's a thriving community blending urban dynamism with rural tranquility, prioritizing sustainable development and inclusive governance for a prosperous future.

Governor's Message

As Governor of Gafarsa Guje Sub-City, it is my honor and privilege to serve this vibrant and diverse community. Our sub-city, home to 271,565 residents and spanning 17,130 hectares, is on a remarkable journey towards becoming a premier hub for innovation and sustainability in East Africa.

  • Our commitment to sustainable development and inclusive governance underpins every initiative we undertake. From pioneering agricultural advancements and eco-friendly urban planning to fostering technological innovation, we are dedicated to enhancing the quality of life for all our residents.
  • I am deeply grateful for the hard work and dedication of our community members, local businesses, and public servants. Together, we are building a future that balances growth with environmental stewardship, ensuring prosperity for current and future generations.
  • Thank you for your continued support and active participation in our shared vision. Together, we will achieve great things and make Gafarsa Guje Sub-City a beacon of progress and opportunity.
