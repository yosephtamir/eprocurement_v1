About Us

Gafarsa Guje Sub-City, a dynamic enclave led by Mr. Ashenafi Chemeda, spans 17,130 hectares with a vibrant population of 271,565. Our ethos centers on sustainable development and inclusive governance, fostering a harmonious blend of urban vitality and rural charm for a prosperous tomorrow. Join us in shaping a brighter future where innovation, opportunity, and community thrive.

Core Values

Learning all the time Working in groups The service Honesty and transparency Encourage Innovation and Sustainability Scientifically driven thinking Excellence and professionalism

Our Mission

Strongly connect government offices, modernize service delivery with technology, expand the center of science and technology centers, increase public knowledge of science and technology, ensure security and data storage to improve the livelihood of the subcity's community.

Our Vision

By 2030, Our Sub-City will be the Event Innovation and Technology Hub in East Africa.
